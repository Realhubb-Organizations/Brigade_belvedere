About Brigade Belvedere Bengaluru Project

Official Pre-Launch Opportunity · By Brigade Group

"A seamless synthesis of high-rise architectural brilliance and vast green low-density enclaves, custom-crafted for modern urban families."

The upcoming Brigade Belvedere development introduces a highly refined residential address in one of East Bengaluru’s most prominent locations. Designed as a landmark high-rise gated community, this premium launch by Brigade Group seamlessly blends modern urban architecture with vast open spaces. Spanning a sprawling 10.75-acre land parcel, the project features five iconic high-rise towers rising up to G+43 floors, offering spectacular views of the city skyline and a green, low-density living environment for modern families.

Featuring a grand clubhouse equipped with world-class wellness, recreational, and sporting facilities, Brigade Belvedere provides a resort-style lifestyle. The residences are designed with maximum carpet area efficiency, boasting smart home provisions, luxury materials, and oversized windows that ensure abundant natural ventilation and ambient light.
